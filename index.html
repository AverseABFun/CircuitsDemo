Information

You can click/drag Numbers, Adders, Multipliers, and Exponentiators onto the canvas. Connect them by click/dragging from an Output (circle with an arrow pointing out) to an Input (circle with side arrows). Change an input value by clicking it and dragging to the left or right.

SELECTION - Select multiple numbers and operators by holding shift and dragging a select box or by holding shift and clicking on them. Clicking on the canvas will deselect all. Clicking on a number or operator without holding shift will deselect all others.

DELETION - Pressing the backspace key will delete all selected items.

REVERSAL - Double click any free input or output to switch between free and bound modes.

ZOOM - Use the mousewheel or the "+" and "-" keys to zoom in and out.
